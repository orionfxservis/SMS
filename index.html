Cohort Design System · v1.0

One quiet system, three points of view.

Admin, Teacher, and Parent all run on the same components, type, and spacing — each portal only changes its accent, never its structure. That's what makes the redesign feel premium and learnable at once: once you know one screen, you know them all.

Portal identity

Every portal shares layout and components. Identity lives only in the sidebar accent, the active nav state, and the avatar ring — enough to orient, never enough to fragment the system.

Admin

Runs the school

Enrollment, staffing, fees, and school-wide reporting. Accent: Signal Amber.

Teacher

Runs the classroom

Attendance, gradebook, and lesson-day tools. Accent: Teacher Teal.

Parent

Follows the child

Progress, attendance, fees, and announcements. Accent: Parent Plum.
